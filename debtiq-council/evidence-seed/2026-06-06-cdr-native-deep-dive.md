# Evidence — CDR-native deep-dive (seam 2 reality check) (2026-06-06)

Type: REAL research (cited). Tests whether "build CDR-native, agentic, auto-
application" is open white space. Honest verdict: the DATA half is OWNED; the
WRITE half is NOT READY. This redirects the thesis from "build CDR-native" to
"orchestrate the owned rails." Confidence per claim.

## Q1 — Does NextGen/Frollo already own the broker CDR flow? YES, the data half. [high]
- **NextGen Financial Passport**: CDR-generated bank statements (gov CDR logo),
  Insights (income/asset/liability/expense), **income regularity scores**, and
  **Automated Income Verification for PAYG** — built INTO ApplyOnline, adopted by
  Connective, Finsure, Mortgage Choice, plus CBA & Westpac.
- **Frollo for Brokers**: free to all MFAA/FBAA brokers, **auto-populates
  applications** with permissioned CDR data, AI categorisation/insights, integrated
  into ApplyOnline (Connective, Finsure, Mortgage Choice).
- Conclusion: "pull verified bank data → insights → auto-populate the application"
  is DONE, free, on the dominant rail, backed by the big aggregators and banks.
  Building DebtIQ's own CDR data-read layer = re-cloning an owned, free incumbent. ✗

## Q2 — What does action initiation (write access) actually allow now? NOT loan auto-submit. [high]
- The Treasury Laws Amendment (CDR) Act 2024 (assent 26 Aug 2024) is only the
  FRAMEWORK. Specific actions must be **declared by the Minister** after consultation,
  then rules made per action type. Nothing is live until then.
- **Payment initiation is the PRIORITY first use case** — not mortgage-application
  initiation. New roles (Accredited Action Initiators / Action Service Providers)
  are **ACCC-accredited**; an AAI generally must also be an Accredited Data Recipient.
- "Redirect to App" standard required only from **10 May 2027**.
- Lending use case cited is "switch lenders / share data at a click" — NOT "an agent
  writes and lodges a full mortgage application."
- Conclusion: building a 2026 business on action-initiation auto-application is
  premature, payment-first, ministerial-declaration-gated and accreditation-heavy. ✗

## Q3 — So what is actually left unowned? The GLUE, not the rails. [medium-high]
NextGen/Frollo do CDR data + auto-populate. They do NOT do:
- Document forensics on the NON-CDR documents (payslips, tax returns, BAS, accountant
  declarations) that self-employed / alt-doc / SME deals depend on (CDR is bank data
  only).
- Multi-lender SERVICEABILITY synthesis (that's Quickli).
- An auto-generated BID / responsible-lending EVIDENCE narrative + audit trail.
No one stitches data + forensics + serviceability + compliance-evidence into ONE
agentic pass. That glue is the surviving white space.

## Redirect (honest thesis correction)
- KILL the "build CDR-native from scratch" sub-bet (seam 2 as stated). The CDR data
  layer is owned (NextGen/Frollo); action-initiation isn't ready.
- KEEP seam 1 (unified agent) realised THROUGH seam 5 (orchestration): sit ON TOP of
  the owned rails — consume NextGen/Frollo for bank data — and add the unowned glue
  (forensics for the alt-doc/SME documents CDR can't verify + serviceability synthesis
  + auto-generated compliance evidence). Aim at seam 6 (commercial/SME), where even
  NextGen/Quickli/Frollo are thin.
- Net: the play is an ORCHESTRATION + COMPLIANCE-EVIDENCE agent, not a CDR rebuild.
