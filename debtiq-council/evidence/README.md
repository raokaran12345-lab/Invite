# Evidence inbox

Drop real-world ground truth here as `.md`, `.txt`, `.json`, or `.csv` and the
council folds it into the **next** round, then archives it to `_archive/`.

This is the only thing that lets the maturity score climb past ~50. Reasoning
alone cannot — by design.

Good things to drop in:

- **Broker interviews** — what they actually said about workflow, trust, pain.
- **Real per-deal AI cost** — measured vision/OCR + forensics + generation spend.
- **Pricing tests** — what a broker said yes/no to, and at what number.
- **Sign-ups / activation / retention** — real usage, not projections.
- **Distribution** — aggregator conversations, signed pilots, intro outcomes.
- **Accuracy** — extraction/forensics hit rate on a real document set.

One finding per file is fine. Be specific and dated — the more concrete, the
more it moves the score and sharpens the red-flag scan.

> This file and `.gitkeep` are ignored by the council; everything else you drop
> here is read once and moved to `_archive/`.
