DEBTIQ — BUSINESS MODEL
Last updated: 2026-06-01

STATUS: PARTIAL — inferred from the build; not formally documented by owner.

INFERRED BUSINESS MODEL
- B2B SaaS for AU mortgage brokers and (separately) lenders' assessment
  teams.
- Two distinct workspaces: broker (deal authoring + submission) and
  lender / assessor (decisioning).
- Coordinates the full deal lifecycle from intake to settlement, but
  HANDS OFF at the conveyancing boundary — does not settle, holds no
  trust money.

USER ROLES (from the RBAC model — likely seat tiers)
- Owner: brokerage / lender principal. Super-admin.
- Admin: manages team and settings.
- Broker: front-office deal authoring (broker side).
- Processor: operational ops (pre-assessment + fulfilment + doc gen).
- Read-only: auditor / observer / external reviewer.

REVENUE LEVERS (inferred — not confirmed)
- Per-seat (matched to RBAC roles).
- Per-deal submitted (audit log captures every deal.submit event).
- Per-pack (submission packs + group packs are discrete deliverables).
- Per-org (lending groups create natural aggregator/brokerage scoping).

CUSTOMER OBLIGATIONS THE BUILD ENABLES (NOT THE OWNER'S OBLIGATIONS)
- Broker's responsible-lending evidence trail.
- Broker's Best Interests Duty (BID) artefact.
- Broker's disclosure document spine.
- Lender's assessment audit trail with attributed actions.

DEBTIQ'S OWN OBLIGATIONS — to confirm with counsel
